CST300: Major ProSeminar

Major ProSeminar, is designed to help students identify and articulate their  personal, professional, and social goals. Additionally, it provides an overview of the entire  program and the exploration of opportunities offered in the tech industry. Students are  encouraged to begin development on professional portfolios, explore career paths and graduate  school options, engage in collaborative projects to develop personal skills and consider  professional ethics in the field. The intended outcome is to instill a spirit of professionalism  through teamwork, integrity, and reliability. Elements of this course are incorporated into the  rest of the program. Many students continue the practice long after achieving their educational  aspirations, highlighting the importance of what is offered by this course.
